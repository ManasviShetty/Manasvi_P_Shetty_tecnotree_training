Tribute to Puneeth Rajkumar

Puneeth Rajkumar was a popular actor, playback singer, and television presenter in the Kannada film industry. He was born on March 17, 1975, and was the youngest son of famous actor Rajkumar. Puneeth Rajkumar made his acting debut in 1976 at the age of six, and went on to act in over 25 films.

Some of Puneeth Rajkumar's most popular films include Appu, Abhi, Veera Kannadiga, and Raaj the Showman. He was also known for his singing talent, and sang several popular songs in his films.

Puneeth Rajkumar was widely loved and respected by his fans and colleagues in the film industry. He passed away on October 29, 2021, at the age of 46, leaving behind a rich legacy in the Kannada film industry.
